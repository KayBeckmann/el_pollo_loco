This is the story of Pepe and El Pollo Loco.

El Pollo Loco is a video game in which the character Pepe fights against chickens and collects coins. He must save the village of Pueblo Pequeno from a horde of wild chickens. Pepe fights his way through the chicken farm, dodging obstacles and collecting coins for upgrades. After defeating the leader of the chickens, El Pollo Loco, Pepe is celebrated as a hero.
